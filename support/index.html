Frequently asked questions

How do I place a flag?

Long-press a tile (or right-click) to flag a suspected mine. Long-press again to remove it. The counter at the top shows how many mines remain unflagged.

Is my first tap always safe?

Yes. The board is generated after your first tap, so your opening move — and the tiles around it — will never be a mine.

How do I change the difficulty or board size?

On the Play tab use the Beginner / Intermediate / Expert buttons, or tap Custom to set your own rows, columns and mine count.

How do I switch themes?

Tap the palette button in the top-right to cycle through the four themes (Aurora, Neon, Ocean, Candy). Your choice is remembered automatically.

Where are my stats and achievements stored?

Everything is saved locally on your device — no account needed. You can clear it any time with the “Reset all stats & records” button on the Stats tab.

Does Boomsweep collect any data?

No. It works fully offline with no ads, no tracking and no accounts. See our Privacy Policy for details.
