Boomsweep — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Boomsweep (“the app”) is designed to respect your privacy completely.
No data collected · No ads · No tracking

What we collect

Nothing. Boomsweep does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data stored on your device

The app saves the following locally on your device only, so your progress persists between sessions:

  • Game statistics (games played, wins, losses, streaks, time played)
  • Best completion times per difficulty
  • Unlocked achievements
  • Your theme and sound preferences

This information never leaves your device. It is removed if you uninstall the app or use the in-app “Reset all stats & records” button.

Network, ads and tracking

  • Boomsweep works fully offline and makes no network requests.
  • There are no advertisements.
  • There is no analytics or third-party tracking SDK of any kind.
  • There are no user accounts or logins.

Children’s privacy

Boomsweep does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new “last updated” date.
